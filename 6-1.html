Sed Magna Ornare

I was exchange student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University during the summer vacation in 2018 summer. I enter USC- SJTU Institute of Cultural and Creative Industry. USC-SJTU Institute of Cultural and Creative Industry (ICCI) was founded in 2015 by Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and is an internationally-oriented school within SJTU. ICCI’s establishment grew from the joint efforts of the University of Southern California (USC), Minhang District Government, and Shanghai Zizhu High-tech (Group) Co., Ltd. As an important aspect and expression of SJTU’s internationalization strategy, ICCI integrates global world-class resources reflecting the demands of the industry’s value chain and the market.

ICCI’s distinction is three-fold – internationalization, interdisciplinary focus, and Industry orientation – and takes on the mission to nurture creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship among talents for the cultural and creative industry. ICCI dedicates itself to being a global institution providing premier resources to aspiring students from all over the world. And I interacted with students there about multimedia field and creativity field.
