Attack on Lazy

Attack on Lazy is a sports game combined with virtual reality. In early 2020, the COVID-19 epidemic swept the world, restricting many crowd activities in order to prevent the spread of the epidemic and causing a significant impact on all kinds of sports activities that require physical contact. Instead, e-sports recovered from the epidemic and became a substitute for all types of sports during the epidemic.

The game appeals to people who are not motivated to play sports and need games to improve their motivation to experience the thrill of the scenes and fights in the world of giants. In addition to being immersed in the world of giants, the most important purpose is to achieve the effect of exercise through stress-free games, there are running, walking, jumping, squatting and other large movements in the game, allowing players to increase the calorie consumption of exercise, the game process to beat five giants, after hitting the success of the level, the final player can get the total amount of calories consumed in the game and the equivalent of several laps of the playground.
