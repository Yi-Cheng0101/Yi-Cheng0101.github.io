Wake up - YI-CHENG HSIAO Art Solo Exhibition

Wake up is a word with a little fun and lazy, but actually, it’s a word that presents the attitude of my life. Wake up means a new start, learning a lesson in life, and maybe it’s a lesson of fault, a process of success, and leaving a loving family. All those can belong to wake up. I’m a person who always makes my best effort to do anything I want to chase. Even though I usually get faults and feel frustrated. After sleep, I will continuously face challenges and have new energy to move on. Hence, I use the word to be the name of my art solo exhibition. This word completely presents my life. This exhibition shows the artworks I painted during college time and it illustrates my various stories in painting ways.
