Interdisciplinary

Fine Art & Computer Science

I aspire to combine computer science and fine art in multimedia.

When I was seven years old, I started learning fine art and studied in an art talent elementary school. During the period of elementary school and junior high school, I was trained in a professional fine art environment, learning deeply many kinds of basic art skills, such as watercolor, sketching, calligraphy, and painting. Because of possessing these experiences, I have strong art skills and a superior vision of aesthetics. In senior high school, I left art talent school and took the normal high school classes,

changing the field from fine art to science. In college, with the ability in fine art and exam scores, I attend the interdisciplinary program at the NTHU (National Tsing Hua University) and choose computer science to study. In college, I continuously sharpen my fine art skills, being a team leader of school art team and attend a number of extracurricular activities. In the future, I plan to combine computer science and fine art in the field of multimedia.
