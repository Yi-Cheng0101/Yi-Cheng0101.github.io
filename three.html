Attack on Lazy

Due to COVID-19, in order to prevent epdemic from spreading, prohibitng people outdoor activities. Hence, people reduce the time to go out for exercise. As a result, exercise gaming boom during the period and have a high speed growth. We used Unity and Oculus to develop a VR game that lets people fitness with different movements at home during covid pandemic. Combined with the anime, Attack on Titan, and VR. People can do different movements to exercise in virtual world.
