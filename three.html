Attack on Lazy

Due to COVID-19, in order to prevent epdemic from spreading, prohibitng people outdoor activities. Hence, people reduce the time to go out for exercise. As a result, exercise gaming boom during the period and have a high speed growth. We used Unity and Oculus to develop a VR game that lets people fitness with different movements at home during covid pandemic. Combined with the anime, Attack on Titan, and VR. People can do different movements to exercise in virtual world.

I'm project manager in our team and coordinated four teammates in design thinking, user flow, artdesign, game design, and programming in a Virtual Reality class. I offered sparkling ideasand leadership to collaborate with my team members. Due to my superior ability in art andcomputer science, I served as a bridge between programmers and designers and offereddirections to our team. I led the team to demonstrate an awesome virtual reality game, named“Attack on Lazy”, and won the second prize in the final project competition.
